The Ritual of Choice

1. The Rinse-Off Mask

For deep restoration and intensive treatment.

  1. Apply generously to clean, damp hair.
  2. Massage through mid-lengths to ends.
  3. Leave for 5-15 minutes (relax and enjoy).
  4. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

2. The Leave-In Therapy

For daily protection, shine, and frizz control.

  1. Apply a pea-sized amount to damp hair.
  2. Concentrate on the lower half/ends.
  3. Do not rinse. Style as usual.
  4. Apply to dry ends to tame flyaways.
