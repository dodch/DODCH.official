Why it’s a Masterpiece

Most shampoos are 90% water and cheap detergents. Glass Glow is different. It is a high-performance treatment formulated around the rarest, most expensive cosmetic oil on the planet: Pure Cold-Pressed Prickly Pear Seed Oil.

At DODCH, we don’t believe in "trace amounts." We believe in results. To create the 10ml of pure oil used in our small-batch production, it required 10 kilograms of hand-selected fruit. Every bottle you hold contains the life-force of over 2,500 seeds, cold-pressed to preserve every drop of Vitamin E and Omega-6.

The Trinity

The Marshmallow Foam

Powered by pure Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate (SCI). Experience a dense, cloud-like lather that cleanses deeply without stripping, respecting your hair’s natural pH of 5.5.

The Liquid Gold

(Prickly Pear Seed Oil): With double the Vitamin E of Argan oil, this "dry oil" doesn't just sit on the surface. It penetrates the hair cortex to repair damage from the inside out, creating a mirror-like "Glass Glow" that reflects light from every angle.

The Scent Journey

A sophisticated "Solar-Floral" signature. The scent opens with the fresh, clean notes of Tunisian Orange Blossom (Néroli) and settles into a warm, lingering heart of Tropical Vanilla and Coconut.

The Ritual

1. The Dosage

Start with a coin-sized amount. For long or thick hair, use two full pumps.

2. The Activation

Rub the cream between your palms with a splash of water to activate the "Marshmallow Foam" before applying to the roots.

3. The Rinse

Massage into the scalp, let the "Solar-Floral" scent bloom for 60 seconds, and rinse with cool water to lock in the Prickly Pear shine.

Founder’s Pro-Tip: The Double Wash

"Because DODCH is sulfate-free and rich in Prickly Pear oil, the first wash focuses on breaking down oils, while the second wash creates the signature 'Marshmallow Foam.' If you don't get a big lather on the first try, don't add more product—just add more water, rinse, and repeat with a single pump. Your hair will stay clean and hydrated for longer."

Concentrated Excellence: Because we use high-grade SCI and no cheap fillers, a little goes a long way. Use sparingly.

The Architecture of Radiance

At DODCH, we believe repair should never feel heavy. Our formula combines rare Prickly Pear Seed Oil with a high-precision, dual-protein blend of Hydrolyzed Wheat and Pure Silk to transform hair from the inside out.

Prickly Pear Seed Oil (The Desert Gold)

The heart of our formula. Packed with Vitamin E and Omega-6, it deeply nourishes and seals the cuticle without the greasy weight of traditional oils.

Scientific Insight

High in Linoleic Acid (Omega-6), which reinforces the hair's lipid barrier, and Tocopherols (Vitamin E), which protect against oxidative stress.

Wheat Protein (Internal Resilience)

These plant-based micro-proteins penetrate the hair’s inner cortex. They act like structural "bricks," plumping the hair from within to restore volume and prevent breakage.

Scientific Insight

Composed of low molecular weight proteins that can penetrate the hair shaft, providing substantive repair and increasing moisture retention.

Silk Protein (The Glass Shield)

Silk possesses the highest affinity for hair keratin. It weaves a microscopic, breathable film over the cuticle, locking in the Prickly Pear Seed Oil and reflecting light for a mirror-like "Glass Glow."

Scientific Insight

Forms a crystalline protective barrier due to its high affinity with keratin. Its molecular structure reflects light for exceptional gloss.
