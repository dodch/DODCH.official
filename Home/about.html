The DODCH Laboratory

DODCH is a Tunisian-based cosmetic house dedicated to the intersection of Mediterranean Botany and Bio-Molecular Science. We do not believe in superficial beauty; we believe in structural integrity. Our laboratory specializes in developing high-performance formulas that utilize bio-mimetic ingredients to repair, protect, and illuminate. Every DODCH creation is engineered to perform under the specific environmental challenges of the Mediterranean climate, delivering professional-grade results through a commitment to purity, potency, and transparency.
